# ADLINK IE Field Guide · Visual Spec

> Updated: 2026-07-22  
> Direction: warm editorial, Claude-inspired, not a direct brand replica

## Identity scope

- Keep the existing text-only ADLINK interview-guide identity.
- Do not use Anthropic or Claude logos, proprietary fonts, or product UI.
- Maintain the existing disclaimer that this is an independent interview-preparation resource.

## Palette

- Canvas: `#faf9f5`
- Surface: `#ffffff`
- Soft surface: `#f0eee6`
- Oat: `#e8e1d6`
- Ink: `#171714`
- Muted ink: `#67645f`
- Border: `#d7d3c9`
- Primary accent: `#c96749`
- Primary accent hover: `#a94f34`
- Dark emphasis: `#24231f`

## Typography

- Display: `Iowan Old Style`, `Palatino Linotype`, `Noto Serif TC`, `Songti TC`, serif
- Body / controls: `Avenir Next`, `Noto Sans TC`, `PingFang TC`, sans-serif
- Detail: `SFMono-Regular`, `Menlo`, monospace

## Design rules

- Mobile reading is the primary scenario; body text remains at least 16px with 1.75 line height.
- Use whitespace and typographic hierarchy before adding containers.
- Reserve clay for actions, key terms and reading progress.
- Use one dark editorial panel per major sequence, not a dark background across the full page.
- All interactive controls have at least a 44px touch target and visible focus state.
